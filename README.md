## Project Description
This project analyzes 2025 sales data to uncover customer purchasing patterns, identify top-performing sales channels, and highlight key opportunities to increase revenue in 2026. Using Microsoft Excel, I built an interactive sales dashboard that allows stakeholders to quickly explore sales performance, customer demographics, and product trends.

## Purpose of the Project
The client wanted to analyze their 2025 sales performance to better understand customer behavior and develop strategies to boost sales in 2026.

## Dataset Used
<a href="https://bit.ly/3X381ok"> Excel Data</a>

## Key Business Questions Answered
This analysis answers several important business questions:
- How do sales and order volumes compare throughout the year?
- Which month recorded the highest sales and orders?
- Which gender purchased more products in 2025?
- What were the different order statuses (delivered, returned, cancelled, etc.)?
- Which states contributed the most to total sales?
- What is the relationship between customer age and gender based on number of orders?
- Which sales channel generated the highest revenue?
- Which product category had the highest sales?

## Project Insights
✔️ Identified March as the top performing month for both sales and order volume.

✔️ Women are the primary customer demographic driving purchases with 29% women more likely to make purchase compared to men

✔️ Established a relationship between age and sales with the Adult age group (30-49 yrs) contributing 50% tot the total sales.

✔️ Discovered the top 5 states contributing the highest revenue made 35% of the total sales

✔️ Found the most profitable sales channel for customer acquisition. 80% of orders were made from Amazon, Flipkart and Myntra channels. 

## Recommendations 
• Target female customers, especially those in the 30–49 age group, since they are more likely to make purchases.

• Encourage customers in low-performing states by offering discounts, promotions, or special deals to increase sales.

• Focus marketing efforts on top-performing product categories to maximize revenue and customer engagement.
